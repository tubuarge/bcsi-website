BCSI-TR program is an online internship program by TUBU and MSKU-BcRG Lab

BCSI-TR program is building an environment where ideas and projects can grow.

The main topic is; Blockchain is much more, than just cryptocurrency.

We aim that BCSI-TR will contribute to the Turkish blockchain ecosystem.


We selected 18 students from all around Turkey.

We should emphasize that BCSI-TR is not a blockchain summer school.

Also, it is a non-profit event.

Experts from all around the world will be attending to share their vision.

The video recordings of the sessions are shared here if the speaker is has given consent: YouTube Link

Program Structure

After giving the basics of blockchain technology, it will focus on developing solutions.

First week will cover the basics of Blockchain development, from Ethereum to Solidty, Concensus algorithms to Smart contract Development and web design

Second week we will start to get project proposal from our students. Also, our Experts will cover topics related their sectors

Third and Fourth week we will continue to host Experts and will help students to learn and finish their projects

Bonus week, we will be presenting student projects in our Demo Day...
