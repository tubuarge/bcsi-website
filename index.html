Blockchain Summer Internship Turkey - 2020

BCSI-TR program is intended to contribute to the Turkish blockchain ecosystem with the help of TUBU and MSKU-BcRG Lab

Blockchain is not a cryptocurrency
BCSI-TR is not a blockchain summer school.
We selected 17 students from all around Turkey. We are building an environment where ideas and projects can grow. Experts from all around the world will be attending to share their vision.
